Who are we, 'polychrome'?
Are you an artist looking to sell your art? Or a place to exhibit your work? We're here to help!

As a team of devoted artists experienced in art marketing and curating business, we want to help rising artists achieve their goals in their artmaking career and assist them in fulfilling their artist vision.

We work closely with our clients to understand your artist needs and goals; That is our top priority here at 'polychrome'.

- What does 'polychrome' mean? -

The word, polychrome, is used to describe having various colors, and we believe that it is exactly what we have. We work with artists who use all kinds of mediums: painting, sculpture, installation, digital media, and more. Each of us working at polychrome has a wide experience in all art forms, making us well fit into providing the best options and advice for newer artists who are beginning their career in the art world.
